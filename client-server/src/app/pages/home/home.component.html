FIND DINING
SCARBOROUGH
Find Dining is a Scarborough based program that celebrates our community's rich, cultural diversity through food. We aim to highlight local and independent restaurants from an array of cuisines.

Using our online platforms, our team helps Scarborough residents and visitors to Find Dining, from neighbourhood favourites to new and hidden gems. Additionally, we help uplift and support our community's restaurants through promotions, programs and workshops.

Find Dining is supported by the City of Toronto, in partnership with the University of Toronto Scarborough and Centennial College.
